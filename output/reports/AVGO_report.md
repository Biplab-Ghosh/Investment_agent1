# Broadcom Inc. (AVGO) Investment Analysis Report

## Valuation Analysis
We employed a Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) methodology to derive Broadcom's intrinsic value. The DCF model is based on the following key assumptions:

- **WACC**: 14.23%
- **Terminal Growth Rate**: 2.50%
- **Cost of Equity**: 14.55%
- **After-Tax Cost of Debt**: 4.44%
- **Beta**: 1.44

Using these inputs, we calculated an intrinsic value of $90.54 per share. This figure is derived from projecting future cash flows, discounting them back to present value using the WACC, and incorporating a terminal value based on the terminal growth rate. The resulting margin of safety of -369.6% indicates that the current market price is significantly overvalued compared to our intrinsic valuation.
